About Me

I am a PhD student in the Faculty of Physics, University of Barcelona, ​​and I have a degree in Mathematics. I love science and teaching. I have over eight years of teaching experience at "Academia Gauss" and as a private teacher, where I have taught mathematics and physics to students of ESO and high school as well as university students.

In 2012 I have become the manager and director of the "Academia Gauss", located in the center of Castelldefels, where I am in charge of management tasks and university classes.

Passionate about scientific dissemination, I am a president and co-founder of the non-profit organization "Quadrivium": an organization dedicated to spreading science throughout the metropolitan area of ​​Barcelona. I also love music and I am studying jazz at Taller de Musics.
